📡 Passive vs Active Sniffing

  • Passive Sniffing: Monitoring traffic on a hub or mirrored port (SPAN) without sending packets.
  • Active Sniffing: Injecting traffic to redirect packets (ARP spoofing, MAC flooding).

🛡️ Sniffing Prerequisites

  • Network interface in promiscuous mode
  • Access to broadcast domain (same subnet)
  • Tools: Wireshark, tcpdump, ettercap

🔓 Vulnerable Protocols

ProtocolPortData Exposed
HTTP80Web traffic, credentials
FTP21Login credentials, files
Telnet23All commands and passwords
SMTP25Email content, credentials
