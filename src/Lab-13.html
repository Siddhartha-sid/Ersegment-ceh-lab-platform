🧱 Firewall Types & Evasion

  • Packet Filtering: Evade with fragmentation, source routing.
  • Stateful Inspection: Use ACK scans, session splicing.
  • Application Proxy: Tunneling over allowed protocols (HTTP, DNS).
  • Next-Gen Firewall (NGFW): Evade with encryption, custom protocols.

📡 IDS/IPS Evasion

  • Signature-Based: Modify attack pattern (obfuscation, encoding).
  • Anomaly-Based: Stay within baseline traffic, slow attacks.
  • Insertion/Evasion: Confuse reassembly with overlapping fragments.
  • Encryption: Hide payload content.
