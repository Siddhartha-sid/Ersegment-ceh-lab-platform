🔑 Symmetric vs Asymmetric

Symmetric Encryption: Same key for encryption and decryption.
Algorithms: AES, DES, 3DES, Blowfish, ChaCha20.
Fast, but key distribution problem.
Asymmetric Encryption: Public key for encryption, private key for decryption.
Algorithms: RSA, ECC, Diffie-Hellman, ElGamal.
Slower, but solves key exchange.

📊 Hashing & Digital Signatures

  • Hash Functions: One-way fixed-length output (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-3).
  • Digital Signatures: Hash encrypted with private key for authenticity and non-repudiation.
  • HMAC: Keyed-hash for integrity and authentication.
