📊 Vulnerability Assessment Lifecycle

  1. Asset Discovery: Identify systems in scope
  2. Scanning: Use automated tools to detect vulnerabilities
  3. Analysis & Prioritization: Assess risk based on CVSS, exploitability, impact
  4. Remediation: Patch, mitigate, or accept risks
  5. Reporting: Document findings for stakeholders

📈 CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System)

Standard for rating severity (0-10):

  • Critical (9.0-10.0): Immediate action required
  • High (7.0-8.9): Patch soon
  • Medium (4.0-6.9): Scheduled patching
  • Low (0.1-3.9): Monitor
